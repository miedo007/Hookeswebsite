## Full Description

Hooked - Crochet & Knitting Row Counter, Patterns & Stash

Hooked makes crochet and knitting simple. Follow patterns step-by-step with plain English explanations, never lose your place with smart stitch tracking, and organize your entire crafting life — all in one app.

Whether you're learning your first chain stitch or tackling a complex cable sweater, Hooked keeps you on track so you actually finish what you start.

### WHY CRAFTERS CHOOSE HOOKED

**Never lose your place again** — Track rows, repeats, and sections with multiple counters. See expected stitch counts per row and get gentle reminders to count every few rows — catch mistakes before they become hours of frogging. Pick up any project exactly where you left off with your row, next instruction, and progress waiting for you.

**Smart Explain** — Stuck on a confusing instruction? Tap any row and get a plain English breakdown adapted to your skill level. No more guessing what "sk 2 sts, (2 dc, ch 1, 2 dc) in next st" means.

**Patterns from everywhere** — Browse 500+ curated patterns, discover new ones from popular crafting sites, import your own PDFs, paste text, or pull directly from your Ravelry library. Switch between US and UK crochet terms with one tap.

**Built for beginners** — 5 guided beginner projects, each with step-by-step video tutorials built right into the app.

**See your progress** — Visual progress bars, progress photos, frogging checkpoints.

**Track your sessions** — Built-in timer on Lock Screen and Dynamic Island, session history.

**Your whole stash, organized** — Log every skein of yarn, hook, needle, and supply.

### FEATURE LIST

- Projects: photos, notes, tags, color-coded folders, status tracking (To Do / In Progress / Paused / Completed), progress bars
- Patterns: 500+ curated (crochet & knitting), web discovery, PDF import with smart parsing, text parsing, Ravelry import, step-by-step Work Mode, US/UK terminology toggle
- Smart Tracking: expected stitch counts per row, count check reminders, multiple counters per project, quick tap increment
- Sessions: timer with pause/resume, Live Activity (Lock Screen + Dynamic Island), history with notes and photos

- Tools: abbreviations glossary (30+ knitting, 30+ crochet), color palette generator, stitch calculator, yarn calculator, unit converter, hook & needle size charts
- Video tutorials: 15 crochet + 32 knitting, basics to advanced
- Smart Diagnosis: describe or snap a photo of your mistake to get help
- Lefty/righty personalized tutorials
